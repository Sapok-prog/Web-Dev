Travel to Puerto Rico

All about Puerto Rico

Puerto Rico is a Caribbean island and unincorporated U.S. territory with a landscape of mountains, waterfalls and the El Yunque tropical rainforest.
In San Juan, the capital and largest city, the Isla Verde area is known for its hotel strip, beach bars and casinos. Its Old San Juan neighborhood features colorful Spanish colonial buildings and El Morro and La Fortaleza, massive, centuries-old fortresses.

Views ♡


Puerto Rico is famous for its breathtaking views that combine tropical beauty with vibrant culture. The island’s beaches have bright turquoise water and soft golden sand, perfect for relaxing or watching the sunset. In El Yunque National Forest, visitors can see lush green mountains, waterfalls, and misty rainforests filled with unique wildlife. Old San Juan offers colorful colonial buildings, historic forts, and stunning ocean views from its stone walls. Whether it’s the glowing Bioluminescent Bay at night or the peaceful countryside hills, Puerto Rico’s landscapes are full of unforgettable sights.

MUST-visit places

Old San Juan

Old San Juan is a vibrant historic district known for its colorful colonial buildings and charming cobblestone streets.
The area is rich in history, with famous landmarks such as Castillo San Felipe del Morro and Castillo San Cristóbal, which once protected the city from invaders.
Visitors can enjoy beautiful ocean views from the fort walls, explore small shops and cafés, and take stunning photos throughout the city.
The mix of history, culture, and seaside scenery makes Old San Juan one of the most memorable places in Puerto Rico.

El Yunque National Forest

El Yunque National Forest is a unique natural treasure and the only tropical rainforest in the U.S. Forest System. It is famous for its lush green vegetation, tall trees, and rich wildlife. Visitors can walk along hiking trails, discover peaceful waterfalls, and enjoy fresh mountain air. The forest also offers scenic viewpoints with breathtaking views of the island, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers and outdoor explorers.
