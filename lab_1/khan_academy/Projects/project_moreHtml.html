Recipe #1

Creamy Buldak Carbonara Ramen

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Samyang Buldak carbonara ramen 1 package
Tteokbokki 5 pieces
dumplings 3
heavy whipping cream 1/3 cupes
egg 1
green onion 1/2

Step 1: Boil noodles, tteokbokki, and dumplings

  • Bring water to a boil in a pot.
  • Add the ramen, 5 pieces of tteokbokki , 3 dumplings , and cook until everything is soft and fully cooked.

Step 2:Drain most of the water

  • Drain the pot, but leave a small amount of water (about 2–3 tablespoons).

Step 3:Add sauce packets

  • Turn the heat off or very low.
  • Add both packets from the ramen
  • Mix well with the noodles, tteokbokki, and dumplings.

Step 4:Add heavy whipping cream

  • Pour in 1/3 cup heavy whipping cream.
  • Stir until the sauce becomes smooth and creamy.

Step 5:Cook the egg

  • Fry the egg separately (sunny-side up or soft yolk).
  • Place it on top of the ramen.

Step 6:Garnish and serve

  • Sprinkle sliced green onion on top.
  • Serve hot and enjoy:)

Source: How To Make A Creamy Buldak Carbonara Ramen

Recipe #2

Uzbek Dimlama

  • Time: 2–3 hours
  • Serves: 6–8 people
Ingredients Quantity
lamb or beef 1 kg
medium potatoes 4–5
onions 2-3
tomatoes 1-2
sweet bell pepper 1
garlic 4-6 cloves
cabbage 1
vegetable oil 2-3 tablespoons
Salt & black pepper to taste

Step 1: Prepare Ingredients

  • Wash and cut all vegetables and meat into large chunks.
  • Separate cabbage leaves — these will be used on top later.

Step 2: Layer the Ingredients

  • Heat the cauldron and pour in the vegetable oil.
  • Put a layer of meat at the bottom, sprinkle with salt, pepper, and spices.
  • Add a layer of onions over the meat.
  • Place carrots and then potatoes on top.
  • Add tomatoes and bell pepper next.
  • Tuck the garlic cloves among the layers.
  • Cover everything with the cabbage leaves — this helps trap steam and keep it juicy.

Step 3: Cook Slowly

  • Close the cauldron with the lid.
  • Cook on very low heat for 1.5–2.5 hours, until the meat is very tender and the vegetables are soft.
  • If the liquids dry up before it’s done, add a little hot water.

Step 4: Serve

  • Carefully remove the cabbage leaves on top.
  • Serve by arranging the layers on a large platter or bowl: cabbage first, then vegetables, then meat. Enjoy:)

Source : ДЫМЛЯМА узбекская в казане на природе

Recipe #3

Pumpkin cream soup

Ingredients Quantity
Pumpkin 700 g
Potatoes 2-3
Carrots 1-2
Onions 1
Garlic 1-2 cloves
Cream(20% fat) 70 ml
Olive oil 3 tbsp
Salt to taste
Water 350 ml

Step 1: Prepare the Vegetables

  • Cut the pumpkin into medium-sized pieces.
  • Finely chop the onion.
  • Cut the carrots into pieces and add a little garlic.

Step 2: Heat the Oil

  • Heat a little olive oil in a frying pan.

Step 3: Sauté the Vegetables

  • Add the onion, carrots, and pumpkin to the pan.
  • Add the garlic.

Step 4: Simmering the Base

  • Simmer the pumpkin with the carrots, onion, and garlic over medium heat for 10 minutes.

Step 5: Preparing the Potatoes

  • Cut the potatoes into medium-sized pieces and place them in the pan.

Step 6: Cooking Until Tender

  • Add the stewed vegetables to the potatoes.
  • Add a small amount of water and cook until the potatoes are tender.

Step 7: Blending the Soup

  • Puree the finished pumpkin and vegetable soup in a blender until smooth.

Step 8: Adding the Cream

  • Pour the cream into the pureed soup and mix well.

Source : Тыквенный крем-суп
